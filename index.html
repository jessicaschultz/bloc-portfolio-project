About.

I'm a St. Louis native living in Southern California who loves learning and creating. My journey into tech is not what you'd typically expect from someone who can build you a responsive site from scratch using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (among other languages, libraries, & frameworks). Like all great things in life, it all started with food...in the shape of a WordPress food blog. To my surprise, I was more fascinated with how I could make the computer do this and that than actually blogging. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs.A site that didn't limit my creativity to a simple lackluster WordPress theme.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized that I found something that channeled my inner creativity and passion for learning, while also giving me the freedom and versatility I was looking for in my work. I realized I found my fire. I love using my passion for design to develop websites that bring to life a client's vision. Rather you're a small startup company, entrepreneur, #girlboss, business professional, or just a blogger with a bigger vision than what your current WordPress theme can handle - I'd love to help you!
